#  EU Product Compliance Checklist  
*A practical checklist to ensure products meet EU Circular Economy, sustainability, and market-entry requirements.*

---

##  1. **General EU Market Compliance**

### **Product Safety**
- [ ] Product complies with EU safety standards  
- [ ] Relevant CE Marking applied (if applicable)  
- [ ] Risk assessment completed  
- [ ] User manual meets EU language and safety requirements  

### **Chemical Safety (REACH / RoHS / CLP)**
- [ ] No restricted chemicals present  
- [ ] Safety Data Sheets (SDS) available  
- [ ] Labelling complies with the Classification, Labelling & Packaging (CLP) Regulation  

---

##  2. **Circular Economy & Sustainability Requirements**

### **Ecodesign for Sustainable Products Regulation (ESPR)**
- [ ] Product is durable and long-lasting  
- [ ] Product is repairable (tools, spare parts available)  
- [ ] Product is reusable or easily disassembled  
- [ ] Product is recyclable or made with recyclable materials  
- [ ] No unnecessary destruction of unsold goods  

### **Environmental Footprint**
- [ ] Material composition documented  
- [ ] Carbon footprint estimated or available  
- [ ] Recycled material content declared  
- [ ] Energy efficiency verified (for electronics/ICT)  

---

##  3. **Digital Product Passport (DPP) Preparation**

*Required for textiles, electronics, batteries, packaging, furniture, and more (rolling out 2026 onward)*

- [ ] Unique product identifier available  
- [ ] QR code / digital label ready  
- [ ] Traceability data collected  
- [ ] Information about materials and chemicals recorded  
- [ ] Repairability score documented  
- [ ] End-of-life instructions available  
- [ ] Sustainability claims supported with evidence  

---

##  4. **EU-Compliant Packaging Checklist**

### **Sustainability Requirements**
- [ ] Packaging is fully recyclable  
- [ ] No banned single-use plastics  
- [ ] Packaging uses minimal material  
- [ ] Recycled content percentage meets EU thresholds  
- [ ] Compostable or biodegradable packaging verified (if claimed)  

### **Labelling**
- [ ] Correct recycling symbols  
- [ ] Material composition labelled  
- [ ] Green claims are verifiable and not misleading  

---

##  5. **Green Claims & Advertising Compliance**

- [ ] No vague terms like *eco-friendly*, *green*, *natural* without proof  
- [ ] Claims backed by scientific evidence or certifications  
- [ ] Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) or similar documentation available  
- [ ] Environmental marketing complies with the Green Claims Regulation  

---

##  6. **Supply Chain Compliance (CSDDD)**

- [ ] Supplier due-diligence performed  
- [ ] No child labour, forced labour, or unsafe working conditions  
- [ ] Environmental risks identified and mitigated  
- [ ] Traceability maintained across all suppliers  
- [ ] Contracts include sustainability obligations  

---

##  7. **Documentation & Technical File**

- [ ] Technical documentation compiled  
- [ ] Certificates of origin and compliance available  
- [ ] Test reports from accredited labs included  
- [ ] Product labels follow EU guidelines  
- [ ] Importer details provided (mandatory for non-EU manufacturers)  

---

##  8. **Customs & Import Requirements**

- [ ] HS code confirmed  
- [ ] Product classification correct  
- [ ] Tariff and duties checked  
- [ ] Required certificates attached  
- [ ] Packaging and documentation meet EU import rules  

---

##  9. **Testing & Quality Assurance**

- [ ] Product tested for safety and durability  
- [ ] Chemical testing completed (REACH / RoHS)  
- [ ] Packaging tested for compliance  
- [ ] Third-party certification obtained (if applicable)  
- [ ] Batch-level quality control documented  

---

##  10. **End-of-Life Compliance**

- [ ] Clear disposal or recycling instructions provided  
- [ ] Take-back system available (electronics, batteries)  
- [ ] Product supports EU circular economy targets  

---

#  Final Status Summary

- [ ] Product fully meets EU safety standards  
- [ ] Product meets circular economy & sustainability requirements  
- [ ] Documentation ready for EU authorities and importers  
- [ ] Supply chain verified for environmental & human rights compliance  
- [ ] Ready for EU market launch
